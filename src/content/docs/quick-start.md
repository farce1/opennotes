This guide walks you through recording your first meeting with openNotes — from setup to viewing your AI-generated summary.

## Step 1: Launch openNotes

Open the app after [installation](/installation/). On first launch, openNotes checks for Ollama and guides you through the initial setup.

<!-- TODO: Screenshot placeholder — first launch setup screen -->

## Step 2: Set up Ollama

If Ollama is running, openNotes will detect it automatically. If not:

1. Make sure [Ollama](https://ollama.com) is installed and running
2. openNotes will prompt you to pull the recommended model (**phi4-mini**)
3. Click **Pull Model** and wait for the download to complete (~2.5 GB)

:::note
You can change the AI model later in **Settings → Summary**. See the [AI Models guide](/guides/ai-models/) for recommendations.
:::

## Step 3: Start recording

1. Click the **Record** button on the main screen
2. Select your **microphone** from the dropdown (or use the default)
3. Start your meeting — openNotes transcribes speech in real-time

You'll see a live transcript updating as you speak. The floating recording widget shows the recording status.

## Step 4: Stop recording

When your meeting ends:

1. Click the **Stop** button
2. openNotes automatically generates a structured summary using your selected AI model
3. You'll be taken to the meeting summary view

## Step 5: Review your summary

The summary includes:

- **Overview** — A brief summary of the meeting
- **Key Points** — Main topics discussed
- **Decisions Made** — Specific decisions reached during the meeting
- **Action Items** — Tasks assigned or agreed upon
- **Title** — An AI-generated title for the meeting

<!-- TODO: Screenshot placeholder — summary view -->

## Step 6: Export or browse

From the summary view, you can:

- **Export** the meeting as PDF, Markdown, or copy to clipboard
- **Browse** your meeting library to find past recordings
- **Re-generate** the summary with a different AI model
