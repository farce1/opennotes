Welcome to openNotes! This guide will help you get started with local-first AI meeting notes.

## What is openNotes?

openNotes is an open-source desktop application that records your meetings, transcribes them offline, and generates structured meeting notes — entirely on your machine.

## Prerequisites

- A computer running macOS, Windows, or Linux
- [Ollama](https://ollama.com) installed for AI summarization
- A working microphone
